# Form Intelligent — Product Vision

`@jayoncode/form-intelligent` is a **headless form workflow engine** for the JOC ecosystem.

## Mission

Orchestrate validation, submission, state, formatting, and multi-step business logic without owning UI rendering.

## Differentiation

Does **not** compete with React Hook Form or TanStack Form on field registration. Focuses on workflows: autosave, drafts, wizards, conditional logic, offline/retry, and JOC integrations.

## Marketing line

**A headless form workflow engine.**
