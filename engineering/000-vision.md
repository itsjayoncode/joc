# 000 Vision

## Why This Document Exists

This document anchors the long-term intent of the JOC ecosystem so repository decisions can be measured against a consistent vision before implementation details begin to compete for attention.

## Vision

JOC should become a durable ecosystem of focused libraries that share quality standards, documentation habits, and architectural discipline without becoming tightly coupled products.

## Repository-Level Goals

- Support many packages over time without losing clarity.
- Make contributor onboarding understandable from the first commit.
- Encourage small, well-scoped libraries instead of broad, unstable abstractions.
- Preserve room for internal reuse without pushing internal code into public packages.

## What Success Looks Like

- New packages fit naturally into the repository structure.
- Contributors understand where work belongs and why.
- Architectural decisions are recorded before scaling pressures make them harder to change.
- The first released package feels like it came from a system, not from an isolated experiment.
